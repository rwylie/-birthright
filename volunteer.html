Volunteer

We love our volunteers.


account_circle

Birthright Counselor

Provides one-on-one crisis intervention and on-going counseling. Makes referrals and follow-up contacts with clients. Maintains accurate and confidential files. Also handles incoming client and business calls. Greets clients; also does telephone counseling, and provides on-going friendship to our Birthright clients.

Usually a four-hour weekly commitment. Training is required and provided.

people

Birthright Counselor Aide

If you don't feel you can counsel just yet you could join a counselor on her shift by doing tests and just generally helping the counselor to spend more time with our clients.

Clothing Ministry

Collects, mends, cleans, irons, and sorts baby and maternity clothing. Variable time commitment.

Seamstress
