Birthright Counselor

Provides one-on-one crisis intervention and on-going counseling. Makes referrals and follow-up contacts with clients. Maintains accurate and confidential files. Also handles incoming client and business calls. Schedules appointments, greets clients; also does telephone counseling, and provides on-going friendship to our Birthright clients.

Usually a four-hour weekly commitment. Training is required and provided.

Birthright Counselor Aide

If you don't feel you can counsel just yet you could join a counselor on her shift by doing tests and just generally helping the counselor to spend more time with our clients.

Clothing Ministry
