How You Can Help

face

Abortion hurts women, as well as their children.

Abortion hurts women, as well as their children. Birthright volunteers are on the front lines here in the Humble area, making a practical, loving effort to help women choose life over abortion. We provide our clients with the wealth of community resources available to help them, at a stressful time that often seems to leave them with no choice except abortion. Mostly, we listen to the young women who come to us, give them free pregnancy tests, and try to help them in whatever ways we can. We strive to be all things to all our clients, so that they might come to know God's love through us. We strive to never judge, but rather to always love unconditionally each one of our Birthright clients.

Birthright of Humble is a 501 (c)3 non-profit organization.
That means that we are completely staffed by volunteers and their efforts, and donations.
